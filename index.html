What to do during quarantine

By Hazel Sandoval
Previous Next

What happened...

‎That was the day I found out that school was suspended and now going to be virtually held. I remember being so happy because the paranoia about the virus was kicking in. If only I knew what the work would be like my opinion would've been so different.

What I did...

‎That was the day I started the workload of work and chatted with my lunch group. It was also the day I started my little mini vlogs called 'Hazel's Isolation Diaries'.

What I spent it with...

‎That day I spent the entire day with my little sister Maribell. We made brunch together, did some challenges, listened to music, and just took pictures. It was a nice memory that I'll cherish.

A Quarantined 20th...

‎April 4th is my best friend Nicole's birthday. I wished her a happy birthday as soon as it turned 12 am in Germany [That's where she lives]. It's one of my favorite days because we talk about how much our friendship has grown and what it means to us both.

A Quarantined 18th...

‎I SPENT MY EIGHTEENTH BIRTHDAY IN QUARANTINE!!! Honestly, it was one of the best birthdays I had. I watched onward [was dying to see it], received unexpected surprises, ate mexican food and played some games with my sisters.

A Quarantined 18th PT.2 ...

‎Haha this time it wasn't my birthday but one of my closest friends Tas'. That day I wished her a happy birthday at 12 am in her time zone. We looked back at our old accounts and one that we used to share together. We also got to video chat which was super cool. I hope she had a wonderful quarantined birthday.
